SEO content and design

Project Info

Following the SEO optimization project and publication of 20 articles on Dr. Hud’s website, I focused on the next phase, enhancing user experience and further boosting SEO performance. This phase involved creating 50 new articles, adding a comprehensive FAQ section, and incorporating visual elements like images and infographics to enrich the articles. Additionally, I worked on designing a search function, refining the layout, and selecting appropriate colors to ensure a cohesive and visually appealing presentation of the articles.

Goals

The main goals of this phase were to improve user engagement by linking articles to one another, ensuring a seamless reading experience, and further enhancing the website’s SEO ranking. By interconnecting articles, I aimed to keep visitors on the website longer, encourage exploration of related content, and improve overall user satisfaction. This phase also aimed to create a more comprehensive and visually appealing article repository on Dr. Hud’s website.

Project development

  • I began by researching best practices for interlinking articles and understanding how this impacts SEO. This included studying successful websites and reviewing tools to assist with this process.
  • Next, I drafted 50 new articles, each carefully crafted with SEO principles such as strategic keyword use, meta titles, and meta descriptions. After drafting, I linked each article to at least two other relevant ones to enhance SEO and improve user engagement.
  • Lastly, I created a comprehensive FAQ section, selected images, designed infographics, and developed a search function. These design aspects were tailored to improve usability, align with Dr. Hud's branding, and further boost SEO.

Final deliverables

By the end of this phase, I created and published 50 new articles on Dr. Hud’s website. Each article was interlinked with other relevant articles, enhancing both SEO and user engagement. I added a new FAQ section to provide quick access to answers for common questions, and incorporated images and infographics to visually enrich the content. The articles were presented in a redesigned layout that includes a search function, improving the overall user experience.

Additionally, I provided guidelines for future article creation, ensuring that all new content adheres to the established SEO principles and design standards.

Summary

The second phase of the SEO optimization project for Dr. Hud focused on interlinking articles, creating 50 new articles, and enhancing the overall design and usability of the website. By interlinking articles, adding a FAQ section, and incorporating visual elements, I aimed to improve user engagement and further boost the website’s SEO ranking. This phase involved careful planning, research, and execution, resulting in a more comprehensive and user-friendly article repository. With the articles now live, the next steps will involve ongoing updates and improvements to maintain and enhance the website's performance.
