Swish feature development, scheduled payments

Project Info

The primary objective of this project is to enhance the Swish platform by introducing a scheduling function for payments. By implementing this feature, Swish aims to empower users with greater control over their finances. The scheduling feature will alleviate the burden of remembering recurring transactions, both incoming and outgoing, thus streamlining the user experience. Additionally, this enhancement will bring Swish in line with industry standards, making it more competitive and appealing to a broader audience.

Goals

The primary goal is to design a feature allowing users to conveniently schedule payments, simplifying their daily routines. Through the scheduled request feature, Swish enables users to automate money transfers from their accounts on specified dates and intervals.

Project development

  • Interviews - Conducted interviews with potential users to gather insights into their pain points and preferences regarding payment management.
  • Empathy Mapping - Utilized empathy mapping techniques to understand users' emotions, behaviors, and pain points related to financial transactions.
  • Flowchart Design - Developed flowcharts to visualize the user journey and identify key touchpoints for implementing the scheduling feature.
  • Wireframes - Created wireframes to outline the visual layout and interaction flow of the scheduling feature, focusing on simplicity and clarity.
  • Prototyping - Developed high-fidelity prototypes to simulate the user experience and gather feedback on the proposed design and functionality.

Final deliverables

Designed an interface that closely aligns with Swish's visual identity while ensuring usability and accessibility for users. Implemented a scheduling feature that is logical and easy to use, based on extensive research and competitor analysis. Conducted user testing on prototypes to validate the effectiveness of the scheduling feature, with feedback indicating a positive reception among users.

Summary

This project aimed to address the need for a scheduling function within the Swish platform, providing users with a convenient and efficient way to manage their finances. Through a comprehensive development process, including user research, design iteration, and testing, the scheduling feature was successfully integrated into Swish, enhancing its value proposition and user experience.
